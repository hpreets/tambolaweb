  • Every month we play Sikh Tambola game where rather than showing 15 numbers on the tambola ticket, we show 15 answers on it.
  • Rather than announcing numbers as we would do in a Tambola, we announce random the question from the below list, one by one.
  • If your ticket has the answer to the current question, you tap on your answer to select it.
  • As soon as you get 5 answers correctly tapped and questions to those 5 answers are already covered, you win Early Five prize.
  • Similarly, you win First Line, Middle Line or Last Line when all answers from your respective lines are selected correctly.
